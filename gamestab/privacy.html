Gamestab Privacy Policy; Last updated January 3rd, 2014.

Gamestab values you and therefore has set up this extensive Privacy-Policy in order to notify you when is data stored, What is being processed and what is being disclosed to other entities.

Though Gamestab is not a US based organization, its privacy policy is in strict compliance with The US Export Authority’s Safe Harbor Principles, is in full and strict compliance with all Israeli privacy regulations.

In order to protect your privacy and make sure you understand exactly what information is stored about you, Gamestab has set up this Privacy Policy. Please keep in mind that while you browse Gamestab's website, no personal Information is retained, but that each software has its own unique privacy policy.

  1. What Personal Information Does Gamestab Store?

    Gamestab collects some personal information, such as your first name, last name, email, birthday and profile picture. Also, Gamestab stores an authentication token from each social network you authorized for your account (such as Facebook or Instagram). Having these access tokens stored means that in some cases, Gamestab may be able to post on your behalf or request information even if you are offline.

  2. What Non-Personal Information Does Gamestab Store?

    Gamestab retains some non-personally identifiable information, such as your approximate geolocation, IP address and other behavioral information relating to your use of the services in order to provide you with better services. Moreover in order to provide you with the services, Gamestab collects your top sites, favorite RSS channels, to-do lists, bookmarks and a list of your recently closed tab.

  3. Are Cookies Used?

    When you use the services, Gamestab may store a cookie on your computer to identify you; such cookie identifies that you previously visited Gamestab's website and allows us to analyze web traffic and provide better service.

  4. How is the Information Processed?

    Gamestab processes your non-personal and personal information and creates statistical and profiling information in order to allow better service and monitor use of the websites. Moreover, Gamestab uses the information to provide you with search results and items on your new tab page.

  5. Who has access to the information?

    Apart from Gamestab's employees and contractors, who are under strict confidentiality and data-protection obligations, no other 3rd party has access to your personal information without your explicit consent. However, some 3rd parties, such as your authorized social networks, do have access to information you use or post through the Services.

  6. 3rd Party Advertisements

    Some of the Gamestab websites have 3rd party services implemented inside them for displaying advertisements and for providing with analytical services. Such 3rd party services, such as Google Adwords may have their own privacy policies.

  7. What to do if you feel your privacy was invaded?

    If you feel that your privacy was invaded, please contact Gamestab directly at team@Gamestab.me and Gamestab’s privacy officer shall investigate and post its report promptly.
